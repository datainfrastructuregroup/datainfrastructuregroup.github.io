<h2 class="h5">Join The Lab</h2>
<p>
  DIG is a research lab and a scholarly community.  If you are interested in our work, you can join us as a student researcher, faculty friend, and/or as a community member. 
</p>

Joining us as a community member means that you will:
<ul>
  <li>be added to our Slack channel, where we discuss current events related to research</li>
  <li>be the first to find out about new projects (and new positions we're hiring for)</li>
  <li>receive invites to all lab events</li>
  <li>receive lab swag </li>
  <li>join a community of Smithies (from many majors) who share your interests</li>
</ul>


<h2 class="h5 mt-4">How to Apply</h2>

</p>
