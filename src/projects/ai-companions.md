## Project Overview

The AI Companions project is an interdisciplinary investigation into how people with AI companions leverage technology to build and sustain their relationships.  

We're still forming research questions and building literature reviews so this is a great project to get involved in if you'd like to join a project early in development and take an active role in it's shaping and publications!
